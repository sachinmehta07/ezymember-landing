How can we help you with EzyMember?

Frequently Asked Questions

Member Management

How do I add a new member and their membership details?
Adding a member is easy! Tap Add Member on the home screen. You can quickly auto-fill their details by selecting them from your phone contacts, or type them manually. Just enter their Name, Phone Number, Start Date, Expiry Date, and the Total/Paid Amount. Once you tap Save, you can even send them a welcome message directly on WhatsApp!
Can I download my member lists for printing or Excel?
Absolutely. You can export your data at any time. Go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data, and choose whether you want a Spreadsheet (CSV) for editing or a PDF for printing. You can even choose to include or hide financial details if you're printing it for your staff.

Renewals & Payments

How do I track who hasn't paid their full fees?
We make it simple to track pending payments. On a member's detail page, you will see exactly what their Total Due and Total Paid amounts are. You can easily record partial or full payments by tapping Collect, and the app will keep a full transaction history for you. You can also filter your member list to show only those with an "Outstanding due."

Reminders

Can I send payment reminders to multiple members at once?
Yes! Save time by using our Bulk Reminder feature. Just go to your "Expiring Soon" or "Expired" tabs, select the members you want to remind, and tap Remind. You can create a ready-to-go message template, and the app will automatically fill in each member's name and due amount before opening WhatsApp for you to send.

Account & Data Security

Will I lose my data if I change my phone? How does backup work?
Your data is 100% private and stays on your device—we never store it on our servers! To keep it safe, go to Settings > Account & Backup and sign in with your Google account. This securely backs up your data to your own Google Drive. If you get a new phone, just log in with the same Google account to easily restore everything.
