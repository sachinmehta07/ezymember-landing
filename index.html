Core Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Know exactly who's expiring, send reminders in one tap, and keep your member data private — all without an internet connection.

Add Members in 2 Clicks

Forget long forms. Enter a name and phone number — done. Your new member is saved instantly.

Smart 3-Tab Dashboard

One glance shows you Active, Expiring Soon, and Expired members. No digging through menus.

Smart Expiry Filters

1-Click WhatsApp Reminders

Polite, pre-filled messages customized with your business name. Send renewal reminders, expiry alerts, or welcome messages — one tap.

Direct Calling

Welcome & Renewal Messages

Automatically send a welcome message with membership details when you add a new member, and a renewal confirmation when they re-subscribe.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No training needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use EzyMember.

Add a Member

Tap the + button, fill in a name and phone number, and hit save. Done in 2 clicks.
