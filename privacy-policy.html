1. Introduction

EzyMember ("we", "our", "the App", or "EzyMember") is an offline-first member management application developed by Builtfirst Labs. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use our application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using EzyMember, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this policy.

2. Data Storage Architecture and Local Isolation

EzyMember is architected to operate primarily as an offline application. Your data security is our paramount concern, and we achieve this through the following mechanisms:

  • Zero Custom Cloud Servers: We do not own, operate, or maintain any external backend servers or custom databases. All data processing occurs exclusively on your device.
  • Local Sandboxing: All personal profiles, customer lists, subscription tracking logs, and financial records you enter are stored locally on your device within the secure Android application sandbox. We have no access to this data.
  • Storage Technologies: Relational data is managed via the Android Jetpack Room database, configuration preferences are stored via Jetpack DataStore, and application launch flags use standard SharedPreferences. These are all native Android storage mechanisms that provide encryption and isolation at the operating system level.
Key Benefit: Because your data never leaves your device, you maintain complete control and ownership of all your information at all times.

3. Android Device Permissions and Usage Disclosures

To provide specific localized features, EzyMember requests the following system permissions. We request the minimum permissions necessary and will never use them for any purpose beyond what is explicitly stated:

Read Contacts (android.permission.READ_CONTACTS)

  • Purpose: This permission is utilized exclusively to allow you to optionally select and import existing contacts from your device into your local EzyMember database. This feature is entirely optional.
  • Privacy Protection: The App processes contact matching entirely on your physical device. We never copy, collect, transmit, or upload your address book data to any external server or share it with third parties.

Network Access (android.permission.INTERNET & android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)

  • Purpose: These permissions are used exclusively to:
    • Check network availability to determine if your device is connected to the internet
    • Communicate with authorized Google and Firebase infrastructure for analytics and crash reporting (as detailed in Section 4)
    • Enable the Google Drive backup feature (as detailed in Section 5)

4. Third-Party Analytics and Telemetry

The App incorporates standard Google developer tools to maintain application stability and improve user experience. These services operate independently and do not share personal data:

Google Analytics for Firebase

Automatically tracks anonymous application telemetry, such as feature engagement, session durations, and user interface interactions. This data helps us understand which features are most valuable to users and identify areas for improvement.

Firebase Crashlytics

Collects anonymized system state details, stack traces, and crash logs upon unexpected application failures to assist in debugging and improving application stability.

Data Exclusion

These services capture hardware metrics and anonymous application events only. They do not have access to, nor do they ever transmit, the underlying names, phone numbers, financial records, customer lists, or any personally identifiable information stored within your local database.

5. Google Drive Integration and Restrictive Scopes

EzyMember includes an automated cloud backup mechanism to protect against device loss or hardware failure. This integration is designed with privacy-first principles:

Restricted Scope Isolation (drive.appdata)

The App requests access exclusively to the restricted https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata scope via the Google Drive API. This architecture ensures that:

  • The App can only access the application-specific folder designated by Google Drive
  • No other files, folders, or data on your Google Drive can be accessed
  • The scope restriction is enforced by Google at the API level

File Structure

The integration is used solely to upload and download a single compressed file archive (backup.zip) containing your local database. This enables you to restore your data in case of device loss or damage.

Access Limitations

The App possesses zero visibility or technical privileges over your general Google Drive account. It cannot view, list, read, modify, or delete your personal files, photos, documents, or any other content on your Google Drive outside the application-specific folder.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy (Limited Use Compliance)

EzyMember's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy. Specifically:

  • We do not transmit your Google Drive backup data to any external or third-party servers.
  • We do not use, sell, or repurpose your Google data for targeted advertising, credit scoring, market research, or any other commercial application.
  • No human operators are capable of reading or accessing this backup data; it is processed purely programmatically by the App to execute database restorations at your direct request.
  • All backup operations are performed with your explicit knowledge and consent through the App's backup interface.

7. Document Exports and File Handling

When utilizing the App's feature to export customer rosters or financial history into PDF or CSV spreadsheets, we employ the following security practices:

Private Cache Handling

Generated files are temporarily written to the application's isolated cache directory (context.cacheDir). They are never saved to public, globally readable folders on the device's file system. This ensures sensitive business data remains protected.

Secure Sharing

Files are passed to other apps (such as email or messaging systems) strictly via androidx.core.content.FileProvider, creating temporary, secure content:// URIs that grant minimal necessary access. This prevents other applications from discovering or accessing your sensitive exported data.

Automatic Cleanup

The App actively deletes expired cache files to ensure no residual business information remains permanently exposed on the local file system.
